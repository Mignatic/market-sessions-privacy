Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-10-08

Market Sessions Candles is a utility app that visualizes global trading sessions (Tokyo, London, New York, etc.). The app does not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive user data.

Data we collect

None. The app does not collect personal information, identifiers, or analytics. It operates entirely on-device. If the app accesses the internet, it is only to fetch public time references or session information. Such requests are not linked to any user identity.

Data sharing

We do not share any data with third parties.

Permissions

  • INTERNET (optional): Used only to retrieve public time references if enabled. No personal data is transmitted.

Children

The app is intended for general audiences and does not target children.

Support

This app does not offer direct support.

Changes

We may update this policy to reflect improvements. Any changes will be posted on this page.
